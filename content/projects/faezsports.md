## FaezSports

Community sports organization serving youth in Scarborough, Ontario.

### What We Do

- **Multi-sport programming** — basketball, volleyball, and soccer leagues
- **100+ youth served** across multiple seasons and age groups
- **Charity campaigns** — fundraising for community causes
- **New U8 soccer program** launched for youngest participants
- **Coach coordination** — recruiting, training, and scheduling

### Impact

- Founded from scratch with zero funding
- Grew to 100+ active participants
- Manage annual budgets, facility bookings, and insurance
- Run charity events and community outreach campaigns
