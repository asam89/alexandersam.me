About Me

I'm a PMP-certified Technical Project Manager who sits at the intersection of strategy and engineering. I translate business goals into actionable, technical outcomes — from enterprise ERP migrations (SAP S/4HANA, JD Edwards) to modern cloud-native architectures.

As the founder of Ignyte Consulting, I run an MSP serving businesses across the GTA. I also founded FaezSports, a community sports organization in Scarborough serving 100+ youth across basketball, volleyball, and soccer.

I build my own tools — from AI-powered Telegram assistants running on local LLMs to automated job application pipelines. I believe the best PMs don't just manage; they build.
